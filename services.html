What We Help With

Explore the various branches of Pakistani law our AI assistant is equipped to help you navigate efficiently and effectively.

Pakistan Penal Code

Understand the specific implications of various criminal offences, their corresponding punishments, and critical legal definitions under the primary criminal code of Pakistan. Ideal for grasping the severity of charges or knowing your rights if accused.

Family Law

Get clear, compassionate guidance on highly sensitive and personal topics such as the procedures for Talaq, Khula, child custody battles, and the complex rules of inheritance according to the specific framework of Pakistani family laws.

Police Procedures

Learn exactly how to properly file a First Information Report (FIR), understand what your constitutional rights are during an arrest, and navigate the procedural steps required for obtaining pre-arrest or post-arrest bail.

Property & Civil

Receive basic, structural guidance on common property disputes, how to handle contract breaches, tenant-landlord disagreements, and an overview of civil litigation procedures in the lower and high courts of Pakistan.

Cyber Crime

In the digital age, know your rights. Get detailed information on reporting online harassment, financial fraud, blackmail, and other related offenses under the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) to the FIA.

General Legal Advice

Have a question that doesn't fit the categories above? Ask anyway. If our highly curated local dataset doesn't have the explicit answer, our powerful AI will synthesize the broader law to generate a comprehensive and simplified response.

The Engineering Behind Our Services

Robust Fallback Architecture

When you utilize LawEase Pakistan's services, you are interacting with a highly optimized two-tier artificial intelligence system designed specifically for the Pakistani legal landscape.

  • Primary Vector Search

    Your query is first matched against our proprietary local dataset using vector embeddings. This dataset contains verified, hard-coded information regarding the PPC, CrPC, and Constitution, guaranteeing 100% accurate responses for common issues.

  • Generative AI Fallback

    If your query is unique or complex (e.g., specific property dispute nuances), the system routes the query to a Google Gemini-powered model explicitly instructed to act as a Pakistani legal assistant. It grounds its responses in web-search data to ensure relevance.
