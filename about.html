About LawEase

Our Mission

LawEase Pakistan was developed as a comprehensive Final Year Project (FYP) with a singular mission: to democratize legal knowledge in Pakistan. We believe that understanding the law should not be a privilege reserved for a few, but a basic right accessible to everyone.

How It Works

Our AI chatbot utilizes a hybrid approach. It first checks a curated, highly accurate local dataset containing common legal concepts (like FIRs, Bail, and the Pakistan Penal Code). If your query is unique, it seamlessly falls back to a powerful AI model instructed specifically on Pakistani Law to generate easy-to-understand guidance.
